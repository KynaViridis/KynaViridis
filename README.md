## 🚀 Featured Showcase Project

### Vehicle Dispatcher Management System (ERP)
*Built with PHP & Laravel*

To solve complex operational bottlenecks for a vehicle dispatcher business ("Despachante") in my city, I architected and developed a complete end-to-end management system. This system handles everything from Detran/PR bureaucratic processes to financial conciliation.

**Key Features Developed:**
* **Automated ATPV Import:** Instantly extracts and populates all process data directly from the uploaded ATPV document, eliminating manual entry.
* **Smart PDF Merge:** Combines multiple uploaded attachments into a single optimized PDF, ready for direct upload to the Detran/PR system.
* **Digital Signatures:** Integrated a tablet-based signature solution for clients to legally sign service orders on the spot.
* **Client Tracking Portal:** A dedicated external view for clients to securely track the status of their vehicle processes in real time.
* **Automated Financials:** A dynamic financial module that automatically calculates and applies specific credit card machine rates and bank fees the moment a payment is settled.
